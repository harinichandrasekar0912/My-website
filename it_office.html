IT OFFICE DESIGN

Timeline:
Designed in my 6th semester (Jan to May 2025)

Location of the site:
Masterplanning, architecture, interiors.

Site Area:
5.8 acres = 24000 sqm

Typology:
hLuxury IT Office

Team:
Harini Chandrasekar & Aryan Thiyagarajan

Design Intent:
This IT-office concept pairs user-centred planning with low-tech sustainability. A volumetric Raumplan stacks programme bands so employees stay visually connected across levels, turning the building into one continuous workspace. Function-specific zones—focus pods, sunken collaboration pits, agile benches, formal meeting rooms—are carved out to match task and privacy needs, easing circulation and boosting productivity. The massing also respects the site’s hydrology: roofs and terraces drain to a porous, “sponge-city” rain garden that recharges groundwater while tempering micro-climate. Minimalist detailing and daylight-tuned louvres complete a workplace that prioritises comfort, efficiency and environmental care.
