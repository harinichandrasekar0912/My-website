ABOUT ME

I am Harini Chandrasekar, an architect passionate about crafting meaningful spaces through minimal and thoughtful design. My approach combines functionality with emotion, always placing user experience at the center.

My journey in architecture has included diverse projects—from institutional campuses to housing typologies—and I strive to blend timeless aesthetics with contextual relevance in each one.

I believe that architecture is not just about buildings, but about how people feel and interact within them. This portfolio is a reflection of that vision.
