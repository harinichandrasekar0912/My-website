ABOUT ME

Hello, I’m an architecture undergrad from Chennai who's trying to sketch out the next decade as I go.

For those of you who haven't yet spent some time with me: some days I’m lighting a scene and wondering when Pixar might call; others I’m poking around Apple’s visionOS playground or crunching load paths until the spreadsheet begs for mercy. On quieter nights I feed a brief’s constraints into an algorithm and watch which layouts stagger out of the gauntlet—usually while sipping tea. All these threads share one fuel: an itch to see numbers, colour, and space snap into lockstep.

Optimistic to a fault, competitive for sport—second place makes me twitchy; first place gifts me imposter syndrome. I chase the podium anyway, promising the ego it can nap later.

Inside my head it’s Inside Out on turbo: Logic drags Creativity on side quests while Anxiety supplies unhelpfully colourful commentary. My favourite pastime is riding a runaway thought just to see where it jumps the tracks; most ideas here began exactly that way.

This page is equal parts progress log and confession booth. Browse, prod, or just wave if the silence gets awkward—odds are I’m unpicking another rogue idea. And yes, corralling all these loosely related projects in one place feels absurdly good, doesn’t it? Welcome to the organised chaos; hopefully it helps launch you onto your own tangent!
